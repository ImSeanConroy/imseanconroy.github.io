UpComing

Mobile App Developer — 2021

It is the experiences in life that matter most. UpComing is an app that helps you remember and count down to all your upcoming events. Whether that be holidays, birthdays, anniversaries, or weddings. See how many years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, or seconds an event is away. The concept was to develop an iOS application that improves on the existing apps already on the App Store—adding features that I deemed were missing, such as the ability to change the unit of time being displayed. I have removed UpComing from the App Store to focus on more significant and useful projects.

Cinematic Timelines

Frontend Developer — 2023

  • www.cinematic-timelines.com

Nowadays, A continuous stream of Marvel movies and shows is being released. With so many movies, shows and characters in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, it can be challenging to figure out what order movies happen in and how various events relate. Especially now with the introduction of the multiverse. The idea behind Cinematic Timelines was to develop a simple web application to visualize the MCU in chronological order. Displaying all the movies, shows and how characters went from one film to another.
